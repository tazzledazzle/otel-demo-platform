# Recruiter Screen Prep — Pearl Health

## 60–90 second “Tell me about yourself”

Use this structure; fill in your details and practice until it feels natural.

---

**Opening (years + current focus)**  
“I’m a software engineer with [X] years of experience. Recently I’ve been focused on [Applied AI / productionizing LLM features], including [RAG, vector databases, Agentic workflows — pick what’s true].”

**Full-stack + ownership**  
“I work across the stack — [backend/Python, APIs, data pipelines] and [frontend if true]. I’m used to owning projects from discovery to delivery and working closely with product.”

**Why Pearl / healthcare**  
“I’m excited about Pearl because [value-based care / improving outcomes for providers and patients / building AI that has real impact in healthcare]. I want to work on products that help primary care succeed in value-based care.”

**Closing**  
“That’s why I’m interested in the Senior Software Engineer (Applied AI) role and would love to learn more about the team and roadmap.”

---

## 2–3 questions to ask the recruiter

Pick questions that show you care about the role and the company; adjust as needed.

1. **Team and roadmap**  
   “How is the Applied AI team structured today, and what are the biggest priorities for the next 6–12 months?”

2. **Success for AI features**  
   “How does the team measure success for AI-powered features — e.g. quality, user adoption, or clinical impact?”

3. **Process**  
   “What does the rest of the interview process look like, and what would you suggest I prepare for the technical and panel rounds?”

4. **Culture**  
   “What do you enjoy most about working at Pearl, and how does the engineering culture support building and shipping AI features?”

---

## Notes

- Keep the pitch to 60–90 seconds; rehearse so you don’t rush or ramble.
- Emphasize: years of experience, Applied AI/LLM (RAG, agents, production), full-stack, and why Pearl/healthcare.
- Have your resume and this doc open for reference during the call.
