# Pearl Health — Application & Interview Prep Checklist

Use this as a working checklist. Mark items as you complete them.

---

## Before you apply

- [ ] Gather experience, AI projects, and tech stack for resume (see [resume-template.md](resume-template.md)).
- [ ] Fill out resume using the tailoring table in the template; keep to one page.
- [ ] Draft cover letter using [cover-letter-template.md](cover-letter-template.md) (3 paragraphs).
- [ ] Update LinkedIn headline and About to align with Applied AI, production ML/LLM, full-stack, healthcare.
- [ ] Proofread resume and cover letter; ensure contact info and links are correct.

---

## Recruiter screen

- [ ] Write and practice 60–90 second “tell me about yourself” (see [recruiter-screen-prep.md](recruiter-screen-prep.md)).
- [ ] Choose 2–3 questions to ask the recruiter and write them down.
- [ ] Have resume and recruiter prep doc open during the call.

---

## Technical exercise

- [ ] Review [technical-prep.md](technical-prep.md): RAG flow, vector DBs, observability/evaluation.
- [ ] Practice LeetCode-style medium problems (arrays, strings, trees, graphs).
- [ ] Be ready to discuss: RAG pipeline design, how you’d evaluate an LLM feature, tradeoffs and testing.
- [ ] Test your setup (browser, camera, mic) if the assessment is live.

---

## Engineering Manager interview

- [ ] Prepare 2–3 STAR stories: Applied AI project, mentoring/cross-team, reliability/performance (see [interview-prep.md](interview-prep.md)).
- [ ] Emphasize ownership: discovery to delivery, balancing speed and quality, working with product.
- [ ] Practice saying “I owned discovery to delivery” with a concrete example.

---

## Panel (technical + behavioral + cross-functional)

- [ ] Review system design outline in technical-prep (e.g. “service that uses LLMs to summarize patient data”).
- [ ] Prepare behavioral examples using STAR; align with collaboration, transparency, impact, quality.
- [ ] Prepare one cross-functional example (working with product, clinical, or compliance).
- [ ] Choose 3–5 questions to ask the panel.

---

## Executive interview

- [ ] Prepare one short example per value: Collaborate to Innovate, Trust Through Transparency, Serious Impact Big Heart (see [interview-prep.md](interview-prep.md)).
- [ ] Nail your “why Pearl” and “why healthcare” narrative.
- [ ] Prepare 2–3 strategic questions (roadmap, how AI supports value-based care, team growth).

---

## General

- [ ] Prepare 4–5 STAR stories total (Applied AI, ownership, mentoring, reliability, cross-functional).
- [ ] Practice concise “about you” and time yourself (60–90 sec).
- [ ] Have 3–5 questions to ask at each stage; adapt per interviewer (recruiter vs EM vs panel vs exec).
